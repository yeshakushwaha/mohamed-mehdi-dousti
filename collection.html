My Collection

The Miracle of Energy

Open the Doors To The Undiscovered

Uncover the power of energy within you. Navigate it to Achieve Success

Motivation

To provoke reasoning within the human capacity and open the unopened doors of critical thinking.

Goals

To inspire readers to actively contribute to positive change, and foster a deeper understanding of life, its struggles, and its successes.

Vision

Mohammad Mehdi envisions a world where his writing serves as a catalyst for transforming how we imagine the world and the way we perceive things.

CHAPTERS

Chapters Synopsis Let's Discover

Chapter 01

What is Energy?

The emphasis in this chapter is on managing energy or time; how to channel their energy magician's power by boosting energy-givers and reducing the impact of energy-drainers.

Chapter 02

Energy Scan

This chapter explores the role of an energy detective, guiding readers on how to identify energy drainers and avoid becoming blindfolded.

Chapter 03

Starting

In this chapter, the importance of starting is discussed, emphasizing it as the most essential phase toward success.

Chapter 04

Success and Failure

In this thought-provoking chapter, the conventional notions of success and failure are challenged.

Chapter 05

Ownership and Energy Levels

The relationship between a sense of ownership and energy levels is examined, emphasizing how the lack of it affects our general enthusiasm for life.

Chapter 06

The Energy

Introduction to the fascinating world of energy exchange, uncovering the secret to mastering the energy equation.
