Privacy Policy

When you visit our website or interact with our content, we may gather information about you. This Privacy Policy describes how we use and protect such information. Simply using our website indicates that you are aware of and agree to the procedures outlined above.

To further improve your surfing experience, we may automatically collect certain non-personal information, such as your IP address, browser type, and device information, using cookies and similar technologies.

Use of Collected Information

With the data we gather, we are better able to respond to your needs, provide the services you've asked for, keep you up to date with important information, and enhance the quality of our site overall. In addition, we may use your contact information to send you information about our writing and publishing projects, including promotional materials and other communications until you notify us otherwise.

There may be external links on our website to other websites or services that we do not own or administer. Neither their content nor their privacy policies are under our control. We advise you to read the sites' privacy policies before submitting any personal data.

Children's Privacy

No one under the age of 13 should use our site. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone we know to be under the age of 13. Please get in touch with us if you are a parent or guardian who has learned that your child has provided us with personal information without your consent.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

This privacy statement may be changed or updated at any moment without notice. If you are concerned about how your personal information is collected, used, and stored, we recommend reviewing this policy periodically.
