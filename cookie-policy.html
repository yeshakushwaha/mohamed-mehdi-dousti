Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how cookies and similar technologies are used on Mohammad Mehdi Dousti's website to improve your experience and gather information about your use of the website. You are agreeing to this policy's cookie usage terms by continuing to use the website.

What are Cookies?

When you visit a website, it may store a little text file called a "cookie" on your computer, smartphone, or tablet. They are commonly implemented to enhance the performance of websites, enhance the user experience, and supply website administrators with useful data.

How We Use Cookies

Among the many reasons why cookies are used on this site are:

  • Cookies that are essential to the operation of the website and your usage of its services are set. Typically, cookies are activated after you log in or fill out a form.
  • Using analytical cookies, we are able to track user behavior on our site and make informed decisions about how to optimize the experience for future users. These cookies do not track your browsing history or gather information that could be used to identify you.
  • Cookies with a functional purpose allow the website to remember your preferences and tailor its features and content to you. For future use, they may keep track of things like your chosen language and login information.
  • We may also use information and features from other sites, such as social networking plugins, on our website. Your use of the content provided by these third parties may be monitored using cookies. Please be aware that the cookies used by other websites are not under our control and are subject to the rules of those websites.

Managing Cookies

The cookie settings in your browser give you a lot of power. Any cookies already on your device can be deleted, and you can enable your browser to alert you when a site tries to store a cookie there. Please be aware, however, that deactivating or blocking cookies may have a negative impact on the website's functioning and performance.

Your Consent
