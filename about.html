About Me

Exploring the Unknown is What Defines Me

I Am

Mohammad Mehdi Dousti.

Author, Entrepreneur & Explorer of Novel Ideas

Mohammad Mehdi Dousti is a successful entrepreneur, businessman, and author. He captivates readers and delivers unique perspectives with his extraordinary storytelling ability and profound thoughts. Mehdi is also a versatile individual with exceptional creative talent as a professional pianist. He effortlessly blends his artistic ability into his commercial ventures, integrating creativity and innovation into his writings and ideas. His love of music and the arts demonstrates his commitment to pursuing broad interests and seeking new personal and professional opportunities.

Experience

20+ years of experience and a track record of out-of-ordinary ideas and mindsets that enable innovation.

Motivation

To provoke reasoning within the human capacity and open the unopened doors of critical thinking.

Goals

To inspire readers to actively contribute to positive change, and foster a deeper understanding of life, its struggles, and success.

Vision

Mohammad Mehdi envisions a world where his writing serves as a catalyst for transforming how we imagine the world and the way we perceive things.
